# 🏨 Atiéra Admin System - User Manual

Welcome to the **Atiéra Admin System**! This guide will help you navigate and use the system effectively.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started
1. **Sidebar Navigation**: Use the sidebar on the left to switch between different modules (Dashboard, Facilities, Reservations, Visitor Logs, etc.).
2. **Collapsed Mode**: Click the menu icon (☰) at the top left to collapse the sidebar and see only icons.
3. **Admin Profile**: Your profile details and email are located at the bottom of the sidebar.

---

## 📋 Visitor Management System
To access this, click **Visitor Logs** from the sidebar.

### 1. Recording a Guest (Time-In)
- Go to the **Hotel** or **Restaurant** tab.
- Fill out the check-in form with the guest's details (Name, Room/Table, Host, etc.).
- Click the **Time-in** button. A success alert will appear.

### 2. Monitoring Activities
- The **Dashboard** tab shows real-time stats and a **Recent Activity** list.
- Recent activities are color-coded: **Green** for Checked-in and **Gray** for Checked-out.

### 3. Time-Out Guests
- Go to the **Current Visitors** table.
- Click the **Time-out** button next to the guest's name.

### 4. Generating Reports
- Go to the **Reports** tab.
- Select the **Venue** (Hotel, Restaurant, or All) and the **Date Range**.
- Click **Generate Report**. The system will scroll down automatically to show the **Report Results**.
- Click the blue **Download Report (PDF)** button to save a copy as PDF.

---

## 🛠️ Maintenance Management
To access this, click **Management** -> **Maintenance** from the sidebar.

### 1. Viewing Logs
- The table shows all maintenance tickets with their **Priority** (High, Medium, Low) and **Status**.
- All data in the table is centered for easy reading.

### 2. Scheduling Maintenance
- Click the **+ Schedule Maintenance** button at the top right of the logs card.
- *Note: This feature is coming soon.*

---

## ⚙️ Account & Security Settings
To access this, click **Account** under the Settings section of the sidebar.

### 1. Managing Users
- In the **Users List** tab, you can view all active administrators.
- To add a new user, click **+ Add New User** (Note: Sensitive actions may require a Security PIN).

### 2. Security Tab
- **Change Admin Password**: Update your master password.
- **Security PIN**: Manage the 4-digit PIN for sensitive system tasks.
- **Audit Logs**: View all security events recorded by the system.

---

## 💡 Pro Tips
- **Hard Refresh**: If the UI doesn't look right, press **Ctrl + F5** to clear the cache and load the newest design.
- **Viewing Mode**: If you see a "Viewing Mode Only" badge, you may need to enter your Security PIN to unlock full editing permissions.
